2022 TWHE Conference

March 27-29, 2022 | Change, Innovation, and Resilience
Austin, Texas

Change, Innovation,
and Resilience

Change, Innovation, and Resilience was the theme of the 2022 TWHE Annual Conference and of its closing panel. Thank you to our panelists, Dr. Victoria Black, Dr. Lisa Jasinski, Dr. Ericka Jones, and Skyller Walkes, Ph.D. for providing relevant and thoughtful insights on how higher education professionals can thrive, innovate, and demonstrate resiliency in these challenging times. #twhe #highereducation #womenlead #leadership #genderequity #intersectionality

Dr. Ruth J. Simmons

THE Dr. Ruth J. Simmons provided a keynote address at the TWHE Annual Conference. Dr. Simmons is the President of Prairie View A&M University and was the first Black woman to serve as a president at an Ivy League institution. A trailblazer and a powerhouse, TWHE was honored to host Dr. Simmons and to hear her words of wisdom, humor, candor, and expertise. #twhe #highereducation #womenlead #Texas #highered

The Path to Presidential Leadership Panel

Did you know that less than a third of U.S. college and university presidents are women? The Path to Presidential Leadership Panel at the TWHE Annual Conference provided thoughtful and practical advice for rising to the highest levels of leadership.

Thank you to Dr. Jennifer Kent, President at Victoria College, Dr. Lesia Crumpton-Young, President at Texas Southern University, and Dr. Montserrat Fuentes, President at St. Edward's University for blazing the trail and sharing your valuable insight! And thank you to Carla Brailey, Ph. D.or leading this lively panel! #twhe #twhe2022 #womenlead #womenexecutives #Texas #highered
