Each year, Texas Women in Higher Education Hosts an annual conference, complete with plenary sessions led by renowned women in higher education, as well as engaging, applicable breakout sessions.

The 2023 TWHE Conference planning committee is in the process of preparing for a meaningful and memorable conference that will take place from March 26-28, 2023 at the Embassy Suites-Denton Convention Center in Denton, Texas.
