Texas Women in Higher Education is grateful for the support received from Texas colleges, universities, and affiliated groups. TWHE does not require membership or dues for participation in any programs or for the use of any services. Institutional and affiliated group sponsorships are critical to providing high-quality and accessible programs to women in higher education throughout the state.

Texas Women in Higher Education’s programs and services are delivered by a volunteer Board of Directors and TWHE members throughout the state. Program costs cover only the expenses of the event and are kept at a level that allows participation from women across the state.

Texas Women in Higher Education welcomes institutional, individual, and affiliate group sponsorships at all levels. Sponsors will be recognized on the TWHE website, the TWHE Annual Conference Program, and within individual programs.
